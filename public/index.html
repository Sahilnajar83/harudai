About HarudAI

HarudAI helps Kashmir's apple, walnut and saffron growers spot crop disease early, in their own language. Take a photo, get a clear diagnosis and treatment plan, and listen to the result in Urdu — built for the realities of orchard and field work, not office dashboards.

"Harud" means autumn — the season of harvest in Kashmir. Our goal is a healthier harvest for every farmer, from Sopore to Pahalgam.
